I've tried those make-your-own ramen seasoning recipes, but they taste nothing like ramen in my opinion. This recipe hits the spot, and there is no MSG or artificial ingredients.

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, nori, menma, and scallions.

ingredients

  • vegetable broth
  • water
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • ground ginger
  • sriracha hot sauce
  • soba noodles
  1. Combine broth, water, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce in a pot; bring to a boil.
  2. Add noodles to boiling broth mixture and cook until noodles are tender yet firm to the bite, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Transfer noodles to serving bowls and top with desired amount of broth.
